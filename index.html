About Joystar Metal

专注精密金属零件加工，以技术驱动品质，以供应链整合创造价值

Precision metal parts manufacturing — technology-driven quality, supply-chain integration for value creation.

东莞市星越五金科技有限公司，成立于2014年，是一家集CNC数控加工、车铣复合加工、冲压钣金、全制程外协管理于一体的精密制造企业。公司位于东莞市塘厦镇，拥有进口及国产生产和检测设备70余台，专业团队80余人，年营收突破4000万元。

Founded in 2014, Dongguan Joystar Metal Products Technology Co., Ltd. is a precision manufacturing enterprise integrating CNC machining, mill-turn, stamping, sheet metal, and full supply chain management. Located in Tangxia, Dongguan, with 70+ machines and 80+ professionals, our annual revenue exceeds 20 million CNY. We serve automotive, 3C electronics, medical devices, industrial automation, and smart home industries.

我们服务的行业涵盖：汽车零部件、3C电子、医疗设备、工业自动化、智能家居等，累计交付各类精密零件超百万件。

We serve automotive, 3C electronics, medical devices, industrial automation, and smart home industries, delivering over 1 million parts cumulatively.
